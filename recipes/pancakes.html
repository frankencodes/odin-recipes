Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft and fluffy breakfast food. They are easy to make and can be eaten with syrup, fruit, or sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and milk, then mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  4. Pour batter into small circles.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip.
  6. Cook until both sides are golden.
  7. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.
