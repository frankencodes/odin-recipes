Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a simple pasta dish served with tomato sauce. It is easy to cook and very popular around the world.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional: grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato sauce and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Drain spaghetti and add it to the sauce.
  7. Mix well.
  8. Serve hot with cheese on top.
