Schnitzel

Description

A crispy, breaded meat cutlet that is fried until golden brown. Schnitzel is a popular dish in Germany and Austria.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half or pound them until thin.
  2. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Prepare three bowls: flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs.
  4. Coat chicken in flour first.
  5. Dip in egg mixture.
  6. Coat with breadcrumbs evenly.
  7. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  8. Fry chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until golden and cooked through.
  9. Place on paper towel to remove extra oil.
  10. Serve hot with lemon juice.
